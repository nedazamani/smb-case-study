# 🚀 Product Case Study: Boosting SMB Onboarding & Engagement
*From High Churn to High Confidence: A Data-Driven Approach at Yektanet*

Welcome! This repository breaks down a strategic product initiative aimed at solving a critical onboarding challenge for Small and Medium-sized Businesses (SMBs) using data, benchmarking, and rapid MVP testing.

---

## 🎯 The Challenge
Despite making up 80% of our user base, SMBs were struggling to launch their first ad campaigns due to a steep learning curve. The data painted a clear picture of the friction:
* **High Churn:** A 70% churn rate after the first campaign.
* **Low Conversion:** Only 18% of sign-ups successfully created a campaign.
* **Critical Satisfaction Levels:** An initial NPS of -16, signaling severe user frustration.

## 🧠 Product Strategy & Experimentation
Instead of building a heavy, resource-intensive feature, we defined a North Star Metric (**Time to First Campaign - TTFC**) and launched two rapid MVPs to A/B test our hypotheses:
* **MVP 1 (Support-Led - Virtual Consultant):** Adding a proactive support layer to guide users and bridge the knowledge gap.
* **MVP 2 (Product-Led - Express Campaign):** A friction-less, 3-question customized setup designed to completely remove cognitive load.

## 📈 The Impact (Key Results)
The data revealed a powerful product insight: **The best kind of help is the one users don't even realize they need.** Removing friction (MVP 2) significantly outperformed adding support (MVP 1).
* 🏆 **NPS Transformation:** Shifted from -15 to a positive +4.
* ⚡ **Drastic TTFC Reduction:** 48% of users launched their first campaign within just 12 hours.
* 💰 **Sustainable Revenue:** Transitioned SMBs from impulsive, budget-burning behaviors to a steady, growing, and long-term ad spending pattern.
